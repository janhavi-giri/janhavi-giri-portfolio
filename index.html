About

I operate at the intersection of semiconductor engineering, AI systems, and data infrastructure, helping teams move advanced AI from experimentation into production-scale deployment.

As Principal Architect and Field CTO for Semiconductor EDA Strategy at NetApp, I focus on end-to-end platforms spanning design, manufacturing, and ecosystem workflows. My work emphasizes GPU-accelerated EDA, manufacturing analytics, and AI-ready data infrastructure—connecting technical depth with business impact.As Field CTO, I lead NetApp's go-to-market strategy for semiconductor EDA, opening net-new opportunities and expanding our presence by establishing NetApp's relevance with emerging buying centers focused on AI infrastructure and GPU-accelerated workflows.

I drive C-level technical conversations across the portfolio, championing customer imperatives while serving as the voice of the field to shape strategic product investments.

I am an active industry speaker and technical leader, with a long-term focus on building scalable, ecosystem-level AI platforms for the semiconductor industry.

As the field evolves, I remain passionate about building partnerships that accelerate AI and high-performance computing adoption across design, EDA, and manufacturing.
